Who is Frank Ocean?

A look into his life and work

Early Life

Born Christopher Breaux on October 28, 1987. Frank Ocean grew upn in New Orleans where he wss exposed to the jazz scene and also his mom's R&B CDs. He always loved music and would work a lot of odd jobs to pay for studio time while he was in high school. He went to the University of New Orleans after graduating in 2005. Unfortunately tragedy hit with Hurricane Katrina and the place where he had been recording was badly flooded. He decided to put his academics to the side and move to Los Angeles. He found big music contacts in Los Angeles and ended up ghostwriting for Beyonce and Justin Bieber. He eventually joined Odd Future and the rest is history.

Music

Frank Ocean got his start in music as a ghostwriter. He later joined rap group Odd Future in 2010 and started working on his debut mixtape. He had an instant hit with his first single Novacane and his mixtape was very well recieved and critically acclaimed. He went to release 3 more albums with Chanel Orange being his first studio album. It was another critically acclaimed project that won him a grammy. He then famously released Endless as a throwaway video album to get released from his deal and released Blonde as an independent artist. Blonde became his most popular and critically acclaimed album with a majority of people calling it one of the albums of the decade.

Fashion

Frank Ocean has always been a prominent figure in the fashion industry. He's always been looked at as one of the best dressed artists in the industries. From award shows to Presidential dinners Frank Ocean has a history of always having an impecceable sense of style at every event. His Met Gala outfits are always talked about as they're always on point as manages to keeps it simple and elegant at the same time. In his later years he's had a more hands on relationship with the fashion industry debuting his jewelry line with homer and releasing his own line with Prada as well.
